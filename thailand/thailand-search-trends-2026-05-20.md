## Executive Summary

Thailand's digital economy is thriving: **40.6M online shoppers**, **88% internet penetration**, mobile‑first search behavior, and a strong preference for social commerce (TikTok Shop + Shopee Live). Key search characteristics:

| Dimension | Thailand Profile |
|---|---|
| Search language | Thai 85%, English 10%, mixed (Thai+Eng) 5% |
| Platform share | Shopee 45%, Lazada 25%, TikTok Shop 20%, others 10% |
| Avg. CPC (Google Ads) | THB 5–25 ($0.14–$0.72) — lowest in SEA |
| Payment preference | Bank Transfer 40%, Digital Wallets 25%, COD 20%, Credit Card 15% |
| Search peak | 11.11 > 12.12 > Songkran (Apr) > New Year (Dec) |
| Price sensitivity | Very high — "ถูกๆ" (cheap) is the #1 modifier |

This report analyzes **9 blue‑ocean categories** with strong growth and low competition, providing actionable keyword lists, modifier patterns, intent breakdowns, seasonality, and competitor keyword opportunities.
