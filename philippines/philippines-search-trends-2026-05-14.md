## Methodology

Based on 9 product categories relevant to the Philippines market, this report analyzes Google Ads search term report dimensions including:

1. **High-Frequency Search Terms** — English + Tagalog bilingual keyword lists
2. **Modifier Patterns** — N-gram level high-frequency modifier analysis
3. **Intent Distribution** — Informational / Transactional / Navigational / Commercial breakdown
4. **Seasonal Trends** — Philippines-specific holiday/seasonal drivers
5. **Competitive Brand vs. Generic Terms** — Brand defense and interception strategy
6. **Negative Keyword Architecture** — Waste identification and tiered deployment

> **Data Notes**: The Philippines has ~85 million internet users with Google search market share >95%. However, a significant portion of product discovery happens within e-commerce platforms (Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop). This report focuses on **Google Ads searchable terms** that complement platform-internal searches. English searches dominate commercial queries (unlike Vietnam/Indonesia where local language dominates), but Tagalog/Taglish queries carry high intent for local-market products.
