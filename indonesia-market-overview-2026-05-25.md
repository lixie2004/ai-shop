## 4. Cross-Border E-commerce Policy Environment

| Regulatory Aspect | Details | Impact on Cross-Borders |
|-------------------|---------|--------------------------|
| **Import Tariffs** | 5-10% on most goods | Reduced to 0-5% under RCEP for electronics, machinery |
| **Customs Modernization** | E-Customs system + e-signature law (2019) | 3-4 day clearance for pre-clearance shipments |
| **Seller Tax** | 0.5% transaction tax (effective 2025) | Affects marketplaces (Shopee, Tokopedia) |
| **Data Localization** | 2025 Data Protection Law | Data stored locally within 72 hours |
| **RCEP Benefits** | Zero tariffs on electronics | Attracts tech/cosmetic exporters |

> **Cross-Border Opportunities**:
> - ASEAN FTA reduces tariff barriers for machinery, electronics, and pharmaceuticals.
> - 0.5% seller tax is a head start for local compliance but manageable.
> - E-Customs pre-clearance is streamlined for sourcing from China/US.

> **Sources**:
> - [1] Reuters, "Indonesia E-commerce Seller Tax", 2025-06-25
> - [2] ASEAN FTA Secretariat, "Digital Trade Rules", 2020
> - [3] Indonesia Ministry of Finance, "PPh 22 E-commerce Regulation", 2024
