## Executive Summary

Singapore's digital ecosystem is **mature**: 98.4% internet penetration, 5.78M users, median age 36.2, 100% urban. The consumer searches with high purchase power and low price sensitivity. Key characteristics:

- **English‑dominant** search (95%+), with some Mandarin and Malay supplementary queries
- **High CPC environment** — average $0.50–1.50 USD across commercial categories
- **Short decision funnel** — high ratio of transactional to informational queries
- **Strong brand loyalty** — navigational queries dominate for established brands
- **Platform preference** — Shopee (47% e‑commerce GMV), Lazada (28%), TikTok Shop (18%), Amazon SG (7%)

This report analyzes search trends across 10 high‑potential categories identified in the matrix, providing actionable keyword lists, modifier patterns, intent breakdowns, seasonality, and competitor keyword opportunities.
