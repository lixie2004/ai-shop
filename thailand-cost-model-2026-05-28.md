# Thailand E‑commerce Cost Model 2026‑05‑28

## Executive Summary
This report presents a preliminary full‑cost model for 10 representative SKUs sold on Shopee/Lazada in Thailand for 2026.  The calculation incorporates **procurement, logistics, duties, platform fees, advertising, warehousing, returns, FX‑hedging and customer fulfilment** to estimate gross and net margins.  All figures are expressed in **Singapore dollars (SGD)**; conversion to Thai baht (THB) or Chinese RMB has been omitted for brevity.

> **Key assumptions**
> - **Procurement cost** is 50 % of the retail price (average wholesale margin of 100 %).
> - **Head‑to‑head freight** (SG → TH) = 3 % of retail.
> - **Import duty + VAT** = 9 % of retail (1 % duty + 7 % VAT + 1 % customs handling).
> - **Platform commission** for Shopee/Lazada (2026) = 10 %.
> - **Tail‑furl cost** (local last‑mile) = 2 % of retail.
> - **Advertising** = 5 % of retail.
> - **Warehousing** = 0.5 % of retail per month.
> - **Returns loss** = 3 % of retail.
> - **Foreign‑exchange reserve** = 2 % of retail.
> - The model is non‑tax qualified and should be refined with firm‑specific data.

## Cost‑Optimization Opportunities
