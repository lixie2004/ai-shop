## 4. Policy & Regulatory Environment
| Element | Detail | Compliance Tips |
|---|---|---|
| **GST** | 7 % on most goods, plus customs duty where applicable | Use tax‑free thresholds (SGD 100) for low‑value e‑goods; register with IRAS as a foreign seller |
| **Customs Duty** | Varies by HS code; duty‑free for low‑value goods below SGD 100 under the Overseas Vendor Registration (OVR) regime.
| **e‑Payments** | SEPA‑like cross‑border flows, local payment methods (PayNow, credit card), mandatory compliance with PDPA for data privacy.
| **Customs Clearance** | Automatic filing via SingPass; importers must file import declarations within 3 days.
| **Other** | **Distance‑sale threshold** – Goods valued at SGD 150 and above require GST filing.
| **Data Protection** | Personal data handling must comply with PDPA; data export requires consent.
