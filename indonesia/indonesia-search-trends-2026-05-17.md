*Key take-aways*
* The top three high-traffic categories are **Digital Products**, **Health & Wellness** & **Fitness**; they have the highest search volume and the lowest cost-per-click.
* **Halal** and **bpom** are the strongest trust signals across all categories, especially in Muslim Essentials, Health & Wellness, and Sustainable Food.
* **Ramadan** drives a 100-150% spike in Muslim Essentials, Health & Wellness, and Maternity Care.
* The most prolific platform leakage occurs on **Shopee/Tokopedia/Lazada**; these terms must be added as *account-level negative keywords* to avoid unwanted traffic.
