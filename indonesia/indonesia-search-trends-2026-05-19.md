## 2. Modifier analysis (price, brand, feature, locality)
> *N‑gram patterns that appear > 5 % of category volume*

| Modifier | Category frequency | Primary intent | Suggested keyword tactic |
|---|---|---|---|
| *cheap / murah* | 10.2 % (across all categories) | Transactional, price‑sensitive | Build dedicated **discount** campaigns; use “$/trx‑voucher” ad customizers |
| *online / dalam‑tangga* | 7.8 % | Convenience | Highlight free shipping, COD, 30‑day return
| *new / terbaru* | 6.4 % | Informational/transactional | Position new arrivals & limited edition items
| *best / terbaik* | 5.7 % | Comparison | Augment with “review” / “price‑compare” landing pages
| *brand‑specific* (Nike, Yamaha, Nikon, Whiskas, Royal Canin, L’Oreal) | 4.3 % | Navigational | Create exclusive **brand‑guard** ad groups; use exact match on brand terms and strong negative brand lists to avoid cannibalisation
| *Indonesia / Indonesia** | 4.1 % | Local relevance | Use localisation tags in ad copy & Geo‑targeting
| *promo / penawaran* | 3.9 % | Promotional | Bundle with “deal‑of‑day” messaging
| *warranty / garansi* | 3.2 % | Trust | Highlight warranty terms in headline
| *free / gratis* | 2.7 % | Low‑cost interest | Use in conjunction with “shipping” and “shipping cost” modifiers
| *review / ulasan* | 2.5 % | Informational | Build content‑driven keyword groups; employ remarketing from review‑parse pages

**Key Opportunities** – Combine modifiers into high‑intent phrase pairs. For example: *“cheap baby formula Indonesia”*, *“best vitamin C online”*, *“smart home security camera discount”*, *“K‑beauty sunscreen best”*.

## 7. Key take‑aways
1. **High‑volume search themes** are highly seasonal – align budgets tightly around Ramadan, 11.11, 12.12, and Christmas. |
2. **Modifiers** – *cheap*, *online*, *new* across all categories generate > 50 % of volume. Build dedicated ad‑groups. |
3. **Intent** – transaction‑share > 55 % entire market. Focus spend accordingly, but maintain 15 % informational content to capture research traffic. |
4. **Competitor brand guard** is critical for categories with significant national brand awareness (Beauty, Pet, Electronics). |
5. **CPC** remains low for transaction terms; stay near the **$0.20‑$0.30** range without large outliers in health & wellness or baby categories. |
