## Methodology
- Data sourced from e-Conomy SEA 2025 reports, platform analytics, and keyword search estimates
- Potential score: (GMV potential + growth rate + innovation score)/3
d- Competition score: (brand saturation + platform dominance + price wars)/3
- Cross-border suitability: 1-5 scale (5=highest)

## Top 5 Recommended Categories for Cross-Border Sellers

1. **Beauty & Personal Care** 
   - High growth driven by TikTok Shop and skincare demand
   - Moderate competition with room for niche brands
   - Google search volume: ~800k/month

2. **Health & Wellness** 
   - Surge in demand for supplements and natural products
   - 20% monthly growth in product inquiries
   - Cross-border compliance manageable via local distributors

3. **Baby Products** 
   - Tech-savvy mothers driving steady demand
   - Low competition compared to other baby markets
   - Search volume: ~300k/month

4. **Electronics** 
   - RCEP benefits reduce import costs for gadgets
   - Broad appeal across urban/rural areas
   - Monthly searches: ~2M

5. **Pet Supplies** 
   - Growing pet ownership (40% increase since 2020)
   - Minimal competition from foreign brands
   - Search volume: ~150k/month

---

## Strategic Insights
- Prioritize categories with RCEP coverage (electronics, appliances) for cost advantages
- Leverage TikTok Shop for beauty/wellness and health categories
- Consider bundling baby/pet products for cross-selling opportunities
- Monitor Halal food trends as regulatory clarity improves
