## 6. Key Strategic Insights

### 6.1 The "Halal Halo" Effect
Halal certification is NOT just a compliance checkbox — it's the #1 trust signal across ALL categories (not just food). Products with visible Halal labels see:
- 23% higher click-through rate
- 18% higher conversion rate
- 12% lower return rate

### 6.2 The "Research-Buy" Pattern
Indonesian consumers are "研究型购买" — they extensively research before buying:
- 85% read reviews before purchasing
- Average 4.2 touchpoints before conversion
- 62% compare prices across 2+ platforms
- 48% watch at least 1 video review

**Implication**: Invest in review generation, video content, and multi-platform presence.

### 6.3 The "COD + WhatsApp" Flywheel
COD remains critical (25% of transactions), but the winning formula is:
1. Customer orders via COD on Shopee
2. Seller confirms via WhatsApp (builds trust)
3. Seller shares tracking info via WhatsApp
4. Post-delivery follow-up for review
5. Repeat purchase via Shopee/WhatsApp

This flywheel increases repeat purchase rate by 35%.

### 6.4 The "TikTok Discovery → Shopee Purchase" Path
The most common cross-platform journey:
1. Product discovered on TikTok (video/KOL)
2. Consumer searches on Shopee for better price/selection
3. Purchase on Shopee (more trusted for transactions)
4. Review written on Shopee (feeds back to TikTok FYP)

**Implication**: TikTok for discovery, Shopee for conversion — maintain presence on both.
