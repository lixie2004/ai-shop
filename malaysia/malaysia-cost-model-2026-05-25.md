# Malaysia Cost Model – 2026-05-25

**Report Date**: 2026-05-25  
**Target Market**: Malaysia  
**Platforms**: Shopee + TikTok Shop  
**Currency**: MYR (Malaysian Ringgit)  
**Exchange Rate Reference**: 1 USD ≈ 4.45 MYR  

---

## Assumptions

| Cost Item | Assumption | Basis |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **采购成本 (Procurement)** | China factory FOB cost | Mid-point of estimated landed cost range |
| **头程物流 (Inbound Freight)** | 6% of procurement cost | Sea freight + local trucking to MY warehouse |
| **关税/进口税 (Import Duty)** | 5% of CIF value | General rate for most consumer goods; Halal food/cosmetics may differ |
| **SST 服务税 (Sales & Service Tax)** | 8% of selling price | SST effective 8% from 2024; applicable to retail sales |
| **平台佣金 (Platform Commission)** | 7% of selling price | Blended: Shopee 6-8% avg 7%, TikTok Shop 4-6% avg 5% → weighted 7% for hybrid model |
| **支付手续费 (Payment Fee)** | 2% of selling price | Online payment gateway fee (FPX/credit card) |
| **广告费 (Advertising ACOS)** | 12% of selling price | New brand entry phase; stabilizes to 8-10% after 6 months |
| **退货损耗 (Return Loss)** | 2% of selling price | Avg return rate 3-5%, half can be resold at discount |
| **本地仓储/配送 (Local Storage & Delivery)** | 5% of selling price | 3PL fulfillment + last-mile delivery |
| **认证分摊 (Certification Amortization)** | 1% of selling price | Halal certification / cosmetic notification / food registration amortized over 12 months |

> **Note**: All values are **illustrative** and should be validated with actual supplier quotes, real-time freight rates, platform fee schedules, and current exchange rates.
