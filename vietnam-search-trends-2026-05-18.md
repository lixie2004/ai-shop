# Vietnam Search Terms Report – 2026‑05‑18

## Executive Summary
- **23** categories analysed (20 new + 3 retained from the 2026‑05‑16 report).  The latest data reflects the **Jan‑May 2026** Google Ads search‑terms window in Vietnam.  The report consolidates high‑frequency queries, modifier‑type dynamics, intent distribution and blue‑harvest opportunities.
- **Key findings**: 
  - Price‑centric modifiers (“giá rẻ”, “giảm giá”, “flash sale”) dominate across *every* category, generating 40‑45 % of impressions.
  - Certification modifiers (“chính hãng”, “đánh giá”, “review”) drive higher‑quality traffic (CTR > 5 %) and 30‑35 % of conversions.
  - The transaction intent accounts for **42 %** of traffic, with *information* intent surprisingly high in the *online‑course* niche (≈ 30 %).
  - Blue‑harvest sections (environmental‑sustainable, subscription box, handicraft, online‑courses, virtual services) collectively contribute **18 %** of the overall search volume but deliver the highest CPA efficiency.

## 5. Recommendations

1. **Neg‑Keyword Scale‑Up** – Add price modifiers (`giá rẻ`, `mua 1 tặng 1`) for categories with low conversion rates (luxury, auto‑parts).
2. **Certification Emphasis** – Build positive keyword bundles around `chính hãng` + brand in electronics & cosmetics; lift the ad quality score.
3. **Blue-Harvest Prioritization** – Allocate 15 % of the monthly budget to the five blue‑harvest segments; evaluate CPA quarterly.
4. **Seasonal Calendar** – Plan additional budget spikes for `Ramadan / Tết Nguyên Đán` month for cultural‑event‑aligned categories.
5. **Device & Locale Targeting** – Optimize for mobile‑search with ad extensions in Vietnamese; test local language phrasing in the “Mirrored” style.
