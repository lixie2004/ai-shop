# Vietnam Cost Model – 2026-05-25

> **Assumptions**
> - Selling prices are set at the midpoint of the price ranges listed in the **Top‑10 SKU Selection** report.
> - Procurement cost is taken as the midpoint of the CIF cost range provided.
> - Head‑end logistics (inbound freight & handling) is assumed to be **5 %** of procurement cost.
> - Platform commission is **8 %** of the selling price.
> - Digital advertising spend is **10 %** of the selling price.
> - Last‑mile delivery cost is **3 %** of the selling price.
> - Return loss (handling, restocking) is assumed at **1 %** of the selling price.
> - Exchange‑rate swing impact is taken as **0.5 %** of the selling price.
> - Tax cost (e.g., VAT, import duties beyond CIF) is approximated at **10 %** of the selling price.
> - All values are shown in *thousand VND* (k VND).
> 
> *All figures are illustrative and intended to provide a concept of the cost structure. Adjustments should be made with actual supplier data and marketplace fee schedules.*
