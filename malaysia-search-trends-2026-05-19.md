## 6. Strategic Recommendations
1. **Core focus** – Baby & Mother, Health & Wellness, Pet Supplies: high search volume, low competition, robust seasonality.  Build dedicated campaigns with high‑intent match types and optimize the budget for the peak months (Apr–Jun, Dec). |
2. **Opportunity layers** – Smart Home, Beauty: concentrate on “cheap”‑aware and “online‑shop” modifiers; use social‑commerce listings on Shopee/TikTok for quick cannibalisation. |
3. **Seasonal pacing** – Allocate 20–25 % of cloud spend to the July‑Aug period for all categories; double spend during 11.11 + 2‑3 month Ramadan promotions. |
4. **Creative themes** – Core brand icons with “only in Malaysia” badges, free‑shipping features and promo‑code CTA. |
5. **Negative keyword list** – Add *free*, *download*, *tutorial*, *repair*, *cheap* (for price‑sensitive) along with **local competitors** for each category where cross‑border terms appear.
