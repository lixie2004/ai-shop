# Singapore Compliance Checklist – 2026-05-25

> **Audience**: Product, Regulatory, QA & Logistics teams
> **Scope**: Top‑10 SKUs for the Singapore market
> **Regulatory environment**: High‑enforcement, consumer‑rights‑driven market with tiered risk for health claims

---

## 1. Regulatory Overview

| Agency | Jurisdiction | Key Regimes |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **HSA** (Health Sciences Authority) | Cosmetics, health supplements, traditional medicines, pharmaceuticals | Cosmetic Product Notification System (CPNS); Health Supplement pre‑market registration; Proprietary Chinese Medicine (PCM) licensing |
| **SFA** (Singapore Food Agency) | All food & food appliances import/export | Food import licence (RIPFP); Per‑shipment permits for meat/fish/eggs/produce; Food‑contact material safety |
| **NParks/Animal & Veterinary Service (AVS)** | Pet food, animal‑related products | Pet food licensing; animal health certification |
| **Enterprise Singapore** | General product safety, consumer goods | Consumer Protection (Safety Requirements) Regulations (CPSR) – mandatory registration of 45 categories; voluntary safety marks |
| **Singapore Customs** | Import/export control, GST, duty | GST registration (9%); duty‑free for most food/cosmetics; TradeNet permit system |

## 4. Cross‑Cutting Requirements

### GST Registration
- All 10 SKUs are subject to **9% GST** on import value + declared freight.
- If total annual import value exceeds SGD $1 million, compulsory GST registration as an importer. Otherwise, use customs COS (Cash‑On‑Shipment) or import via a licensed agent.

### MUIS Halal Certification (where applicable)
- Singapore's halal authority is **MUIS (Islamic Religious Council of Singapore)** – NOT JAKIM.
- Cost: ~SGD $500–$2,000 per product category (varies by complexity & ingredient audit).
- Cycle: **4–8 weeks** including ingredient verification and facility inspection.
- Key: Products must source only MUIS‑recognised halal ingredients.

### Labelling Requirements (All SKUs)
- English language mandatory; bilingual (English + Malay/Chinese) recommended for consumer‑facing market.
- Importers' full name & address in Singapore.
- Net weight/volume, expiry date, batch number.
- Country of origin.
- No false or misleading claims (CPFTA).

### TradeNet Permits
- Every commercial shipment must be declared via TradeNet (Singapore Customs portal).
- SFA‑controlled products require an import permit number.
- Penalty: SGD $10,000 per unauthorised shipment.

## 6. Alternative Compliance Strategies

## 7. Quick Reference: HSA vs SFA vs Others

| Decision Point | HSA (Health Supplements) | SFA (Food Products) | Enterprise Singapore (General Goods) |
|----------------|--------------------------|---------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Pre‑market approval | Required (6–12 months) | Licence only (~2–4 weeks) | None (except 45 CPSR categories) |
| Fee | SGD $750–$2,500 | SGD $105/year | $0–$500 |
| Dossier required | Full safety, efficacy, stability | Ingredient list + GMP | Test report (if CPSR) |
| Enforcement | Criminal + product recall | Permit suspension + fines | Civil recall + fines |
| Risk for health‑claim product | High | Medium | N/A |
